# Categories in the EDOS Dataset

## label_category

| Category | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **1. threats, plans to harm and incitement** | Direct threats or encouragement of violence against women. |
| **2. derogation** | Descriptive attacks, aggressive/emotive attacks, dehumanizing attacks, sexual objectification. |
| **3. animosity** | Gendered slurs, stereotypes, backhanded compliments, condescending explanations. |
| **4. prejudiced discussions** | Supporting mistreatment of individual women or systemic discrimination. |
